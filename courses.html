IGCSE IGCSE Sciences & Mathematics”

I offer one-to-one IGCSE tutoring in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology — conceptually rigorous courses framed around the Cambridge curriculum. Lessons emphasize logic, inquiry, and deduction, helping students move beyond memorization to genuine understanding. Real-life phenomena, historical context, and cross-disciplinary examples make abstract concepts tangible and meaningful. Sessions are flexible and personalised, with tailored revision plans, problem-solving practice, and creative note-taking strategies. Supplemented with documentaries, storytelling, and visual tools, this approach cultivates critical thinking and builds confident, independent learners ready for exam success and beyond..

Introduction to Critical Thinking & Scientific Wonder

This course explores the nature of knowledge, science, and imagination. We engage with diverse materials including essays and stories from Carl Sagan, Loren Eiseley, Alan Lightman, Primo Levi, and Jacob Bronowski — each offering poetic, philosophical, and humanistic perspectives on science and discovery. Students develop critical comprehension, reflective habits, and the ability to extract deep insights from complex narratives and media.
