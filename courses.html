I offer a select number of deeply engaging courses, designed to foster critical thinking, interdisciplinary awareness, and genuine intellectual curiosity.

IGCSE Chemistry

A conceptually rigorous course framed around the Cambridge IGCSE curriculum. Emphasis on logic, deduction, and making chemistry relevant through real-life phenomena and historical context. Supplemented with documentaries, problem-solving, and creative note-taking techniques.

IGCSE Biology

This course emphasizes conceptual clarity and scientific thinking within the IGCSE Biology syllabus. Students explore life sciences through inquiry, storytelling, and cross-disciplinary examples. The teaching approach integrates real-world biological phenomena and helps students connect molecular biology to ecology and human physiology.
